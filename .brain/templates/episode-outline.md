## Structure

**Episode Title:** [Short, punchy — what someone would tell a friend about this video]

**Pillar:** Building in Public | Sales Leadership | AI x Sales | Tools & Discoveries

**Hook (first 3 seconds):**
- One sentence that stops the scroll
- Pattern: surprising claim, contrarian take, or "I just did [unexpected thing]"

**Talking Points (60-90 seconds):**
- 3-4 bullet points, each 1-2 sentences
- Concrete, not abstract
- One personal story or real example
- Build to a point — don't just list things

**Closing (last 10 seconds):**
- Restate the core insight in one sentence
- Optional: ask a question to drive comments

**Companion Post (for andrewdonato.com):**
- TL;DR (2-3 sentences)
- Full Take (expanded version, ~200 words)
- Resources (links to anything mentioned)
- Question (engagement prompt)

## Derivation Rules

- Read the source knowledge document(s)
- Find the angle that's most walkable — something Andrew can talk about naturally while moving
- Must be expressible in 60-90 seconds — if it needs more, it's two episodes
- Prioritize personal experience over abstract analysis
- Apply LinkedIn exclusion rules from audience-profiles.md
