# EliseAI — Sales Leadership Opportunity

**Status:** In process as of April 8, 2026.

## What EliseAI Does

Agentic AI platform that automates communication and operations for **housing** and **healthcare**. Handles messages, calls, and tasks across voice, SMS, email, and chat — 7 languages spoken, 51 written.

**Housing (core business):**
- Automates the entire resident lifecycle: prospect management, AI-guided tours, lease signings, maintenance requests, rent collection, renewals, delinquency workflows
- Powers 10% of the U.S. apartment market
- 75% of NMHC's Top 50 Operators are customers
- Clients include Brookfield, Greystar, AvalonBay, Equity Residential, Bozzuto, Invitation Homes
- One customer reported $14M in payroll savings from 1.5M automated interactions/year

**Healthcare (expansion):**
- Patient scheduling, intake, reminders, billing, clinical communication automation
- OB/GYN, dermatology, ophthalmology, orthopedics

## Why This Is a Compelling Fit

**1. Real estate + AI.** Andrew is building MSA for luxury real estate brokers. EliseAI serves multifamily real estate operators. Different segments but the same industry. Andrew would be developing deep AI-in-real-estate expertise that directly feeds back into MSA's understanding of the market.

**2. This is a rocket ship.** Unicorn valuation, $100M+ ARR, $250M Series E led by a16z, 10% of the U.S. apartment market. This isn't a startup hoping to find product-market fit — they've found it and they're scaling hard. A sales leadership role here is a "ride the wave" opportunity.

**3. NYC-based.** 33rd Street in Manhattan. Accessible from Long Island. Geography works.

**4. The product-sales convergence.** EliseAI's product IS sales automation — AI agents that handle prospect conversations, tour scheduling, lease signings. Andrew would be selling a product that embodies his thesis about product and sales converging. He'd understand the product at a level most sales leaders couldn't.

**5. Vertical AI play.** Like MSA, EliseAI is a vertical AI product (not horizontal). Andrew would be learning how to sell vertical AI at scale — lessons that directly apply to scaling MSA.

**6. Comp potential.** Series E, $100M+ ARR, unicorn — this likely pays well. Could meaningfully move the needle on the $15k/month target and the $100k debt.

**EliseAI is the safer, more MSA-aligned play.** Real estate domain, proven traction, unicorn comp.

Both are AI-first companies in vertical markets. Both are better than staying at TrackForce for the career trajectory Andrew wants.
