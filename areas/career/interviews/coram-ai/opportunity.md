# Coram.ai — Sales Leadership Opportunity

**Status:** Inbound outreach received April 8, 2026. Exploring.

## What Coram Does

AI-first physical security platform. Turns existing IP cameras into intelligent security endpoints. No hardware swap needed — pure software layer on top of existing infrastructure.

**Key products:**
- Natural language video search (search camera footage by describing what you're looking for)
- AI security agents for threat detection (firearms, slip-and-fall, PPE violations)
- Access control compatible with existing door readers
- Emergency management with panic buttons and response coordination
- Visitor management and license plate recognition

## Company Details
- Founded 2022
- $30M total funding ($13.8M Series A led by Battery Ventures / Marcus Ryu)
- Thousands of sites deployed
- Fortune 500 clients, heavy in education, warehouses, healthcare, manufacturing
- 4.9/5 on G2, multiple G2 awards
- Pricing not public — demo/trial model

## Why This Is a Compelling Fit

**1. Domain overlap with TrackForce.** Andrew already knows physical security operations inside and out. TrackForce handles the guard management side (scheduling, certs, billing). Coram handles the AI/camera intelligence side. Same buyer universe, complementary products. Andrew walks in with instant credibility and domain knowledge.

**2. AI-first company.** This is exactly the "frontier industry" Andrew said he wants to move into. Coram isn't a legacy company bolting on AI — they're AI-native. Computer vision, natural language search over video, automated threat detection. This is real AI product work.

**3. Sales leadership at a growth-stage startup.** Series A, $30M raised, clearly scaling (thousands of sites). A VP Sales role here would mean building and leading the go-to-market at a critical growth stage — which is exactly what Andrew does well.

**4. The product/sales convergence thesis.** Coram's product is AI-driven, sold to security-conscious buyers who need to be educated on what's possible. This is the exact convergence Andrew talks about — the salesperson who deeply understands the AI product wins.

**5. Potential bridge, not a detour.** Even if the long-term goal is self-employment, Coram could be a 2-3 year play that: pays well, deepens AI expertise, builds network in AI/security, and provides stability while MSA and other projects mature.
