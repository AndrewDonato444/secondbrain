# "Pre-Call Research" Claude Skill — Building Out Loud Topic

Andrew shared this as a topic for the Building Out Loud video series.

## Raw Content (from screenshot)

Someone describing how their entire sales team is "claude pilled" — they all use a custom Claude skill called "pre-call research."

Setup: Everyone connected Claude to Google Calendar, Crustdata MCP, and Slack.

Before every call it automatically:
- Looks at who's attending
- Pulls the company's page + domain data
- Checks the inbound booking context
- Fetches enriched real-time profiles of each attendee via Crustdata
- Creates a full brief: company snapshot, talking points, prospect assessment

Can be scheduled: "every morning at 8am, run pre-call research on all my calls for the day and DM me the briefs"

Key quote: "its pretty insane that this workflow alone could have been the main feature of a sales saas not even 1 year ago and now its just something anyone can run on claude"
