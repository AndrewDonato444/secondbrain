# Writing Queue — andrewdonato.com

> The pool of writing topics for the site's writing section. Posts publish every other day via scheduled task `publish-andrewdonato-writing`. After a post ships, its topic moves to **Published** and a new one gets added to **Backlog → Active 8** to keep the queue full.

## Guardrails (read every time before writing)

- **Nothing private.** No family details beyond what's already public, no personal finances, no health, no relationship specifics about real named people.
- **Nothing project-specific.** No MSA, TrackForce, Facts Unlocked, Knox Brothers, Sales Edge, ScrollProxy, urcooked, trend-jacker, or any current client/employer specifics. Generalize the lesson.
- **Murky → skip and document.** If a topic's framing is unclear, the angle feels forced, or the post would require info from a private source — DO NOT write it. Move it to `needs-review.md` in this folder with a one-line note about why and pick the next topic in the queue.
- **Voice.** Andrew's voice — see style notes in `areas/personal/private/book-for-ellie.md`. Conversational, story-first, one-line punches, no bullet lists in the prose, lowercase title in the file but title-case in the H1, lands a lesson plainly after earning it. ~600–1100 words. No "in conclusion." No corporate softening.
- **Tone.** Musings. Same energy as Building Out Loud — thinking out loud, not pronouncing from a mountain.
