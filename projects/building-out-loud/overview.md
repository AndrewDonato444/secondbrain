# Building Out Loud — Video Series + Wiki

## Concept

Video series where Andrew builds in public — showing the tools, workflows, and thinking behind what he's creating. andrewdonato.com becomes a companion wiki: super simple, organized markdown plus resources. Watch a video, go to the site to go deeper.

## Wiki Vision

- Markdown-based, minimal, fast
- Organized by topic (not just episode)
- Resources, links, tools mentioned in each video
- Authentic to how Andrew actually works

## First Topic: Pre-Call Research Claude Skill

From X post (screenshot, April 10 2026) — a sales team built a Claude skill that:
1. Connects to Google Calendar, Crustdata MCP, Slack
2. Before every call: pulls attendees, enriches profiles, checks company data, creates a full brief
3. Can be scheduled: "every morning at 8am, research all my calls and DM me the briefs"

The poster's key observation: "this workflow alone could have been the main feature of a sales SaaS not even 1 year ago and now its just something anyone can run on claude"

### Why This Is Perfect for Episode 1

Andrew lives at the intersection of sales and product. He manages a sales team at TrackForce. He's already built automation around Gong coaching. This pre-call research skill is:
- Directly in his wheelhouse (sales + AI automation)
- Something he could build live on camera
- A proof of concept for the thesis: product and sales are converging
- Immediately useful to his own team

### Potential Episode Arc
- Show the X post as the hook
- Build the pre-call research skill live
- Connect it to Google Calendar, Apollo (Andrew already has Apollo), Slack
- Demo it running on real upcoming calls
- Publish the skill + resources to the wiki
