## What This Project Is

**Manager Forge** is a 6-week cohort-based program for AI-enabled sales managers. SaaS/software managers who know their job is changing but haven't built the operating system for it yet.

**The stack model (locked):**
- **AI Spine** — 6 live sessions (90 min each), one per week. AI is the operating context, not a feature.
- **Foundations Library** — best of the original 13-module archive as async companion content.

**Andrew's credibility angle:** He's a VP of Sales who *builds* the AI systems himself (AutoLinkedIn, ScrollProxy, MSA platform) and manages a team of ~10 reps with them in production. Every session teaches from inside the problem.

**Archive location:** `projects/manager-forge/archive/` (inside this vault) — do not edit this. Read only.
