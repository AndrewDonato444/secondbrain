## 📋 Curriculum Overview

Manager-Forge is a comprehensive, 12-module leadership development program for new sales managers in luxury real estate, SaaS, and high-performance sales environments. Each module is designed as a standalone yet interconnected learning experience with video instructor scripts, interactive learner materials, and discussion-based cohort engagement.

**Target Audience:** First-time managers (0-12 months in role)  
**Program Duration:** 12 weeks (one module per week) or self-paced  
**Total Video Runtime:** ~750 minutes (~12.5 hours)  
**Delivery Format:** Hybrid (async video + live weekly Zoom discussions)

---

## 🎯 Module Structure (Standard Template)

Each module includes:

### **MODULE-SCRIPT.md** (Instructor/Facilitator Guide)
- Timestamped video instructor script (~8K-10K words, 60-66 min natural pacing)
- 5 sub-modules per module (12-15 min each)
- Per sub-module: opening hook → teaching points → real examples → tension points → closing summary
- Ready for video production; includes pacing notes, talking points, pauses for reflection
- Embedded frameworks, templates, sample conversations, and real-world examples

### **LEARNER-PACK.md** (Student Workbook)
- 10 worksheets (2 per sub-module; diagnostic + actionable)
- 4-5 self-assessment tools with scoring rubrics
- 5 detailed role-play scenarios with setup, dialogue, and debrief questions
- 5 weekly Zoom discussion prompts (copy-paste ready for facilitators)
- All materials are production-ready, downloadable, printable

### **BRIEF.md** (Curriculum Design Document)
- Complete module design, frameworks, key teaching objectives, tension points
- Real-world examples and stories baked into script/learner materials
- Success metrics for learner mastery
- References to related modules in the curriculum

## 📚 The 12 Modules

### **PILLAR 1: FOUNDATIONAL LEADERSHIP (Modules 1–4)**
*Emotional foundations, organizational navigation, communication, and identity*

#### **Module 1: Emotional Leadership Playbook** (12.5K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Understanding emotional archetypes, managing emotions in high-stress environments
- **Key Frameworks:** Emotion/Performance duality; emotional archetypes; psychological safety
- **Core Insight:** You cannot change people's emotional styles; you leverage their existing momentum
- **Real Examples:** Andrew's team emotional types; reps thriving in right emotional conditions
- **Skills Built:** Emotional intelligence, team dynamics assessment, trauma-informed leadership

#### **Module 2: Managing Up Without Micromanaging Down** (12K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** The translator role; navigating between reps' needs and leadership's demands
- **Key Frameworks:** Translator role (inform, advocate, execute); credibility over likability; proactive communication
- **Core Insight:** Trust with leadership is as important as results; your credibility = currency
- **Real Examples:** Andrew's forecast credibility building; when to advocate vs. execute
- **Skills Built:** Executive communication, stakeholder management, political savvy

#### **Module 3: Difficult Conversation Choreography** (12.5K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Firing, performance plans, critical feedback, comp plan changes
- **Key Frameworks:** Cumulative conversations; no surprises framework; adulthood respect model
- **Core Insight:** Conversations aren't isolated; they're cumulative; foundation matters more than execution
- **Real Examples:** Firing conversation after clear process; performance plan handled with dignity
- **Skills Built:** Difficult conversation structure, truth-telling, feedback without shame

#### **Module 4: Manager Identity Shift** (12K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** The grief of transitioning from individual contributor to leader
- **Key Frameworks:** Identity continuum (0% to 100% manager); grief cycle (5 stages); skill translation
- **Core Insight:** You're not a rep anymore; trying to stay in the game makes you average at both roles
- **Real Examples:** Andrew's burnout from keeping territories; team performance improving after letting go
- **Skills Built:** Identity reframing, grief navigation, letting go of individual contributor identity

---

### **PILLAR 2: RHYTHM & OPERATIONS (Modules 5–7)**
*Structure, systems, and data-driven management*

#### **Module 5: Operating Rhythm** (8.4K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Building predictable cadences for team visibility, coaching, and leadership alignment
- **Key Frameworks:** Three-tier operating rhythm (weekly 1:1s, team meetings, monthly reviews); early signal detection
- **Core Insight:** Consistency > perfection; rhythm prevents crises before they start
- **Real Examples:** Andrew's rhythm rebuild; team performance improvement from predictable meetings
- **Skills Built:** Meeting hygiene, rhythm design, signal detection, team consistency

#### **Module 6: Effective 1:1s** (9.2K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** The most underestimated tool: one-on-one coaching conversations
- **Key Frameworks:** Safety pyramid (5 layers); listening for what's unsaid; coaching sequence (situation → reflection → ownership)
- **Core Insight:** Reps only open up if they feel safe; coaching is asking, not telling
- **Real Examples:** Sally's breakthrough after creating safety; Marcus's stuck deal unstuck through curiosity
- **Skills Built:** Deep listening, coaching question frameworks, emotional safety, accountability without shame

#### **Module 7: Pipeline Review & Forecasting** (9.5K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Using pipeline as your #1 coaching and visibility tool
- **Key Frameworks:** Pipeline stage framework; conversion rate math; accurate forecasting (science + art); early signals
- **Core Insight:** Pipeline is destiny; accurate forecasting = credibility
- **Real Examples:** Andrew's forecast accuracy earning autonomy; Jamie's qualification problem revealed in pipeline shape
- **Skills Built:** Pipeline reading, conversion math, scenario forecasting, rep coaching from data

---

### **PILLAR 3: COACHING & GROWTH (Modules 8–9)**
*Developing people, managing underperformance, and building teams*

#### **Module 8: Coaching Reps to Results** (9.2K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Structured coaching for improvement, recognizing uncoachability, exit decisions
- **Key Frameworks:** Coachability assessment (5 traits); 60-90 day coaching window; behavior-focused coaching; PIP framework
- **Core Insight:** You cannot want someone's success more than they want it; coachability > experience
- **Real Examples:** Sarah's improvement trajectory; Marcus's bad hire recovery at day 90; Jamie's PIP success
- **Skills Built:** Coachability diagnosis, behavior-focused feedback, 60-90 day planning, exit conversations

#### **Module 9: Hiring & Team Building** (9.2K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Structured hiring, onboarding, and building balanced teams
- **Key Frameworks:** Capability-Coachability-Fit framework; 4-part interview structure; 30-60-90 onboarding; team archetypes
- **Core Insight:** Hiring wrong is expensive; onboarding determines success; balance > homogeneity
- **Real Examples:** Likability hire failure; coachability hire success; clone team failure → balanced rebuild
- **Skills Built:** Structured hiring, coachability assessment in interviews, 30-60-90 onboarding, team composition strategy

---

### **PILLAR 4: CULTURE & SUSTAINABILITY (Modules 10–11)**
*Long-term team health, retention, and manager wellness*

#### **Module 10: Motivation & Culture** (8.8K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Building genuine culture (not theater), removing demotivators, intrinsic motivation
- **Key Frameworks:** Culture audit (stated vs. actual); five demotivators (clarity, fairness, growth, connection, trust); motivation spectrum
- **Core Insight:** You can't motivate people; you remove demotivators and create conditions for intrinsic motivation
- **Real Examples:** Culture gap revealed; demotivator fixed; retention improving; remote culture intentionality
- **Skills Built:** Culture audit, demotivator removal, intrinsic motivation building, ritual design, remote team connection

#### **Module 11: Time Management & Scaling** (8.5K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Delegating effectively, automating busywork, protecting focused time, preventing burnout
- **Key Frameworks:** Time audit (core work vs. delegatable vs. automatable); high-value delegation (3-time method); burnout prevention
- **Core Insight:** You cannot scale personal effort; scale team effort; burnout = performance issue
- **Real Examples:** Time audit revelation (30% automatable); delegation success (teaching peer coaching); burnout recovery timeline
- **Skills Built:** Time audit, high-value delegation, automation strategy, focus time protection, burnout prevention/recovery

---

### **PILLAR 5: LONG-TERM LEADERSHIP (Module 12)**
*Career trajectory, executive presence, and sustainable growth*

#### **Module 12: Long-Term Leadership Growth** (CAPSTONE) (8.4K words + 10 worksheets)
- **Focus:** Building a career (not just holding a job); executive presence; navigation and politics; mentors and sponsors
- **Key Frameworks:** Growth paths (IC → manager → manager of managers → director/VP); executive presence (5 components); mentor vs. sponsor
- **Core Insight:** Your first year is about learning your role; the next five are about learning your industry; build intentionally
- **Real Examples:** Growth path clarity; executive presence building; mentor impact; staying good as you grow
- **Skills Built:** Career trajectory planning, executive presence, organizational navigation, mentor/sponsor cultivation, values alignment
