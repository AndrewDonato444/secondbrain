# Manager Forge — Curriculum Roadmap (12 Modules)

**Framework:** Building blocks for first-time SaaS sales managers (0–12 months)  
**Release Order:** Foundational → Operational → Advanced (subject to market research feedback)

---

## Module Sequencing & Dependencies

### Phase 1: Foundation & Mindset (Modules 1–3)
*Prerequisites for all downstream modules*

| # | Module | Status | Focus | Key Outputs |
|---|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | **Emotional Leadership** | ✅ DONE | Understand emotional contagion, rep dumps, boundary-setting | Script, worksheets, assessments |
| 2 | **Managing Up** | ✅ DONE | Filter info upward without eroding team trust | Scripts, comms templates |
| 3 | **Difficult Conversations** | ✅ DONE | Performance issues, vents, escalations | Role-plays, conversation frameworks |

### Phase 2: Operating Systems (Modules 4–6)
*Depends on: Phase 1 foundation*

| # | Module | Status | Focus | Key Outputs |
|---|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| 4 | **Manager Identity Shift** | ⏳ PLANNED | Rep → leader mindset, role clarity, dual responsibility | Identity audit, transition checklist |
| 5 | **Operating Rhythm** | ⏳ PLANNED | Weekly/monthly cadence: 1:1s, team meetings, reviews | Rhythm templates, calendar blueprints |
| 6 | **Effective 1:1s** | ⏳ PLANNED | 1:1 structure, coaching, accountability | 1:1 agenda templates, coaching rubrics |

### Phase 3: Performance & Team (Modules 7–9)
*Depends on: Phases 1–2*

| # | Module | Status | Focus | Key Outputs |
|---|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| 7 | **Pipeline Review & Forecasting** | ⏳ PLANNED | SaaS pipeline mechanics, metrics, forecasting accuracy | Review frameworks, dashboards, scripts |
| 8 | **Coaching Reps to Results** | ⏳ PLANNED | Structured coaching for underperformers, growth, quota | Coaching model, role-plays, frameworks |
| 9 | **Hiring & Team Building** | ⏳ PLANNED | Recruitment, onboarding, team composition | Hiring checklists, onboarding playbooks |

### Phase 4: Culture & Scaling (Modules 10–12)
*Depends on: Phases 1–3*

| # | Module | Status | Focus | Key Outputs |
|---|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| 10 | **Motivation & Culture** | ⏳ PLANNED | Building culture in remote/hybrid SaaS teams, morale, retention | Culture blueprints, motivation frameworks |
| 11 | **Time Management & Scaling** | ⏳ PLANNED | Delegation, automation, scaling yourself, preventing burnout | Delegation frameworks, time audits |
| 12 | **Long-Term Leadership Growth** | ⏳ PLANNED | Career trajectory, exec presence, next-level leadership | Reflection tools, growth planning |

## Content Architecture (Each Module Includes)

- **BRIEF.md** — Learning objectives, key concepts, real examples, sub-modules
- **MODULE-SCRIPT.md** — Video-ready instructor script (3–5 sub-modules, 10–15 min each)
- **LEARNER-PACK.md** — Worksheets, self-assessments, role-plays, Zoom discussion prompts
- **RESEARCH.md** (if applicable) — Neuroscience, industry data, competitor references

**Total output per module:** ~5,000–7,000 words across all deliverables

## Build Waves (Recommended)

**Wave 1 (DONE):** Modules 1–3 (Foundation) — ✅ Complete  
**Wave 2 (NEXT):** Modules 4–6 (Operating Systems) — 3 modules, ~1 hour agent time  
**Wave 3:** Modules 7–9 (Performance & Team) — 3 modules, ~1 hour agent time  
**Wave 4:** Modules 10–12 (Culture & Scaling) — 3 modules, ~1 hour agent time

## Notes & Decisions

- **Threading:** Emotional dumps + managing up + operating rhythm should appear in nearly every module (these are the secret sauce)
- **Tone:** Conversational, battle-tested, zero corporate fluff
- **Examples:** Use real manager quotes, Reddit threads, industry data where available
- **Adaptability:** Each module should work standalone or as part of full program
