# Difficult Conversation Choreography — Learner Pack
version: 1.0 | date: 2026-02-13

## Worksheets (2 per sub-module, 10 total)

### Sub-Module 1: Cumulative Conversation
1. **Conversation Pattern Map**  
   List 5 recent interactions with a rep. Note: What was said? Trust built/eroded? Pattern?  
   [Table: Date | Interaction | Trust Impact | Notes]

2. **Foundation Check**  
   Rate 1-10: Consistency in 1:1s? Honest feedback? Follow-through? Action: One change this week.

### Sub-Module 2: Don't Underestimate Adulthood
3. **Spin vs Real Audit**  
   Recall 3 feedback instances. Spin version? Real version? Impact? Rewrite one as real.

4. **Truth Phrases**  
   For your team: Bad news scenarios. Write 'real' script (data-focused, no softening).

### Sub-Module 3: Data-First Framework
5. **Framework Practice**  
   Pick real situation. Fill: DATA | STORY | AGREEMENT | OUTCOME | EXPECTATIONS | EMOTIONS.

6. **Language Drill**  
   5 phrases per step. Practice aloud.

### Sub-Module 4: Real Conversations
7. **Scenario Prep**  
   For each 4 types: Outline framework application.

8. **Script Builder**  
   Customize scripts for underperformer/firing/comp/perf plan.

### Sub-Module 5: Never Break Trust
9. **Trust Audit**  
   Rate self on 7 breakers/builders. Weakest area? Plan.

10. **Consistency Commitment**  
    Weekly checklist: 1:1s kept? Follow-through? Explanations?

## Self-Assessments (5)
1. **Conversation Credibility Diagnostic** (10 Qs, score): How real are you?
2. **Foundation Strength Check**: Trust per rep (1-10).
3. **Framework Readiness**: Can apply Data-First?
4. **Difficult Conversation Confidence**: Rate 4 types.
5. **Trust Protection Plan**: Actions for consistency.

## Role-Play Scenarios (10)
1. Underperformer (pre-plan).
2. Firing (direct).
3. Comp plan (math).
4. Perf plan (formal).
5. Data pushback.
6. Post-plan follow-up.
7. Critical feedback.
8. Tough decision explain.
9. Emotional response.
10. Proactive 1:1 (foundation).

**Partner role-play: Use framework. Debrief: What worked?**

- Hardest convo avoided? Why?
- Spinning vs real?
- Consistency story?
- Cumulative meaning?
- Prep change?
