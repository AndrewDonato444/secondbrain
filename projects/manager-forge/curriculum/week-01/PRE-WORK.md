## What You're Doing and Why

Week 1 opens with you already having done the thinking — not cold. The session goes deeper when everyone has a baseline in hand. This pre-work gives us a shared starting point so Andrew isn't explaining what you could have read; he's building on what you already know.

Don't skim this. Show up with real answers.

## Step 1: Read the Module 13 BRIEF (10 min)

In the Classroom tab → Foundations Library → Module 13: The AI Manager → BRIEF

Read the full brief. You don't need to memorize it. You need to understand the core argument:

- What "role compression" actually means for managers
- The Both/And framework (not AI or human — both, in the right places)
- What "abdication" looks like vs. smart delegation
- The "lazy work" concept and why it's a management problem, not a tools problem

**Come ready to answer:** What's the one sentence from this brief that hit you hardest?

## Step 3: Complete WS-13-02 — What's At Stake in Your Role (15 min)

In the Classroom tab → Foundations Library → Module 13 → Learner Pack → Worksheet 2
