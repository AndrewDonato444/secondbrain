# Week 2 Pre-Work — Pipeline Reviews That Don't Waste Everyone's Time

**Manager Forge — AI Spine**  
**Complete before Session 2**  
**Time required:** 30–40 minutes

---

## What You're Preparing For

Session 2 is about redesigning your pipeline review from a status-gathering meeting into a coaching conversation. We're going to do a live workshop using your actual pipeline data — conversion rates, rep performance, deal shapes.

This means you need to come with your numbers. The work below takes 30–40 minutes. Don't skip it — managers who bring real data get significantly more out of the workshop than those who estimate.

---

## Step 1 — Read the Foundation (15–20 min)

Open the Module 07 Foundations Library in the Skool community:  
**Pipeline Review & Forecasting — Foundations**

Read these two sections only (not the full module):
- **Sub-Module 1: The Pipeline Architecture** — Pipeline stages and what each one tells you about rep performance
- **Sub-Module 3: The Pipeline Health Diagnostic** — The four unhealthy pipeline patterns: too many early-stage deals, stuck deals, wrong deal sizes, low close rate

Skip Sub-Module 4 (The Weekly Review Meeting) — that's what Session 2 replaces.

**What you're looking for:** Vocabulary and pattern recognition. When we do the live workshop, you should know what "stage inflation" and "pipeline shape" mean without needing an explanation.

---

## Step 2 — Calculate Your Conversion Rates (15–20 min)

Pull your actual pipeline data for the last 90 days. You'll need:
- How many deals entered "Qualified" (or your equivalent) in the last 90 days
- How many of those closed (won or lost)

**WS-07-03: Calculate Your Company Conversion Rate**

```
Deals closed (won + lost) in last 90 days: _______
Deals that were "won": _______
Conversion rate: Won ÷ Total closed = _______%
```

If you don't have 90 days of data yet, use whatever you have. If you inherited a team, use the team's historical rate.

**Questions to sit with before the session:**
1. Which rep's conversion rate surprises you?
2. Is there a pattern — do certain deal types, deal sizes, or territories convert at different rates?
3. What's your own estimate of why the lowest-converting rep is converting at that rate?

Bring these numbers to Session 2. You'll use them in the workshop.

---

## Optional (but valuable): Look at Your Last Pipeline Review

If you can pull up notes or a recording from your most recent pipeline review, spend 5 minutes on this:

- How much of the meeting was the rep talking through deal status?
- How much was you actually coaching on a specific problem?
- What was your opening question?

You don't need to write anything up. Just notice the pattern so you have a concrete before/after reference point for Session 2.

---

## What to Bring

- Your company's conversion rate (from WS-07-03)
- Each rep's conversion rate (from WS-07-04)
- A general sense of your team's current pipeline shape (even a rough mental picture: "mostly early-stage," "concentrated in a few big deals," etc.)
- One deal you're not sure about — something where your gut says "I don't know if this is real or not"
