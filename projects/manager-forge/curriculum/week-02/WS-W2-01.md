## The Standard You're Aiming For

Before this session: *"Walk me through your pipeline."*  
After this session: *"I reviewed the summary. Here's what I saw. Let's spend our time on these three."*

That shift doesn't require a better tool. It requires 30 minutes of prep and knowing where the tool is blind.
