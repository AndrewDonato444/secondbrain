# Week 3 Pre-Work — Coaching the Rep in the Age of AI Reps

**Manager Forge — AI Spine**  
**Complete before Session 3**  
**Time required:** 30–45 minutes

---

## What You're Preparing For

Session 3 introduces three specific AI-related coaching archetypes: the Over-Reliant Rep, the AI-Avoider, and the AI-Native with Other Gaps. We'll do live coaching role-plays in the session, and you'll leave with a single-rep coaching plan written out.

To get the most out of the workshop, you need to come with two things: your coachability diagnostic already run on one rep, and a clear picture of which rep you're going to build your coaching plan around.

The pre-work takes 30–45 minutes. Don't skip the SA-08-01 step — the live workshop builds directly on it.

---

## Step 1 — Read the Foundation (15–20 min)

Open the Module 08 Foundations Library in the Skool community:  
**Coaching Reps to Results — Foundations**

Read these two sections:
- **The Coachability Assessment** — the framework for distinguishing reps who want to improve from those who won't
- **The Behavior-Focused Coaching Framework** — why you coach observable behaviors, not personality traits or vague feedback like "be better at discovery"

Skip the 60-90 Day Coaching Window section for now — you'll use that in the live session.

**What you're looking for:** A shared vocabulary. In the workshop, when Andrew says "coachability signals" and "behavior vs. personality coaching," you should know exactly what that means without needing a definition.

---

## Step 2 — Run the Coachability Diagnostic (15–20 min)

**SA-08-01: Coachability Diagnostic**

Find the SA-08-01 self-assessment in the Module 08 Foundations Library.

Run it on one rep — specifically the rep you suspect has an AI-related performance gap. Don't pick your easiest rep. Pick the one who's been in the back of your mind since Session 1.

Work through the assessment honestly. The coachability score will affect which archetype matters most and how you open the coaching conversation.

**Bring your score and answers to Session 3** — you'll use them in the Coachability Matrix workshop block.

---

## Step 3 — Observe One Thing Before the Session (5 min)

Between now and Session 3, do one observation on the rep you chose:

Pick one of these — whichever is most natural given your operating rhythm:

**Option A — Outreach review:** Look at one email or sequence this rep sent in the last week. Ask yourself: "Could this have been sent to anyone, or does it reflect specific knowledge about this prospect?"

**Option B — Pipeline check:** Look at this rep's deal updates from the last two weeks. Do they reflect real conversations, or could they have been generated without actually talking to the prospect?

**Option C — Conversation count:** How many real human-to-human prospecting conversations did this rep have this week? Not emails sent — actual conversations where a prospect talked back.

You don't need to do anything with what you see yet. Just notice it. You'll bring it to the session as a data point when we build the coaching plan.

---

## What to Bring

- Your SA-08-01 coachability score for one rep
- The rep you want to build WS-W3-01 around (name, role, general performance context)
- One observation from Step 3 — what you saw, no analysis needed yet
- A rough sense of whether this rep's issue is "using AI too much," "not using it at all," or "using it fine but still missing"
