# Week 4 Pre-Work — The 1:1 and Team Meeting That Actually Works Now

**Manager Forge — AI Spine**  
**Complete before Session 4**  
**Time required:** 30–40 minutes

---

## What You're Preparing For

Session 4 redesigns your 1:1 format from the ground up — specifically, how to incorporate AI-generated prep data without letting it flatten the conversation into a data review. We'll do the Safety Pyramid assessment live for your actual reps, and you'll leave with a new 1:1 format to run next week.

Two things to bring: your 1:1 effectiveness audit (SA-06-01) already completed, and a clear picture of the safety level you actually have with each rep — not where you hope you are, where you actually are.

---

## Step 1 — Read the Foundation (20 min)

Open the Module 06 Foundations Library in the Skool community:  
**Effective 1:1s — Foundations**

Read these two sections:
- **The Safety Pyramid** — the five-layer model for psychological safety in 1:1s. Know all five levels before the session; we'll use them in the live workshop.
- **The Coaching Question Sequence** — how to structure 1:1 questions so you're asking, not telling. The sequence you build in Session 4 will use this structure.

Skip the "Accountability Without Shame" section for now — that's the Session 4 closer, and we'll cover it live.

**What you're looking for:** A working model of safety before we build the AI protocol on top of it. The AI-integrated 1:1 only works if you understand the safety foundation it has to sit on.

---

## Step 2 — Complete SA-06-01: 1:1 Effectiveness Audit (10 min)

Find SA-06-01 in the Module 06 Foundations Library.

Complete it for your current 1:1 approach — honest assessment of what your 1:1s are actually doing, not what you intend them to do.

Pay specific attention to the questions about:
- How much of the meeting is status vs. coaching
- Whether reps are bringing real problems or sanitized versions
- Whether you're walking in prepared or cold

**Bring your audit results to Session 4.** You'll use them in the Safety Pyramid workshop.

---

## Step 3 — One Observation Before the Session (5 min)

Between now and Session 4, notice one thing in your next 1:1 with any rep:

What did you walk in knowing — and where did that knowledge come from? The AI tool, your own notes, something you observed in the field, something from the week before?

You don't need to change anything. Just notice your current prep process with fresh eyes. You'll have context to contrast when we build the new format in Session 4.

---

## What to Bring

- Your SA-06-01 completed
- A read on where each of your direct reports currently sits on the Safety Pyramid (rough estimate — 1-5 per layer, per rep)
- One rep in mind for the workshop exercise: ideally someone whose 1:1 isn't working as well as you'd like, or where you feel like you're not getting the real conversation
- Optional: one recent 1:1 moment where the data drove the conversation in a direction you wish it hadn't
