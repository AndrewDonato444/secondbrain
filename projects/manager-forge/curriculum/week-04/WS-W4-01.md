# WS-W4-01 — My AI-Integrated 1:1 Format

**Manager Forge — AI Spine | Week 4**

**Use:** Fill in before your next 1:1. Run it once. Post in the Skool Week 4 thread.

---

## What This Is

A one-rep 1:1 prep template that integrates AI-generated data with the human context no tool can provide. You fill this in before the meeting. It becomes your prep — not your agenda.

The meeting is still yours. This just makes sure you walk in knowing what you know.
