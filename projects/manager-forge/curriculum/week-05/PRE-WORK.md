# Week 5 Pre-Work — Hiring for the Team That Doesn't Exist Yet

**Manager Forge — AI Spine**  
**Complete before Session 5**  
**Time required:** 30–40 minutes

---

## What You're Preparing For

Session 5 adds AI fluency as a fourth hiring dimension and builds an AI ramp track into your 30-60-90 onboarding plan. We'll practice the AI probe questions live using the Sarah interview role-play, and you'll leave with a concrete update to your interview process before your next hire.

To get the most from the workshop, you need your current hiring framework clearly in mind — including what you currently screen for and where your onboarding plan does and doesn't have structure. The pre-work surfaces that picture before we update it.

---

## Step 1 — Read the Foundation (15 min)

Open the Module 09 Foundations Library in the Skool community:  
**Hiring & Team Building — Foundations**

Read these two sections:
- **The Hiring Assessment Framework (Capability-Coachability-Fit)** — the three-part model your existing interview process should be built on. Know all three dimensions cold before Session 5; we're adding a fourth.
- **The Four-Part Interview Structure** — the standard interview arc. Know where each part sits so you can identify where the AI probe questions fit naturally.

Skip the Onboarding and Team Composition sections for now — we'll cover the AI ramp additions live.

**What you're looking for:** A clear baseline of your current hiring approach so you know exactly what you're updating, not starting from scratch.

---

## Step 2 — Complete the Pre-Work Assessments (15 min)

**WS-09-01: Design Your 4-Part Interview Structure**

Map out your current interview process — even if it's not fully structured. Where does skill assessment happen? Where do you probe coachability? Where do you evaluate fit? Identify the gap: where would an AI probe question fit?

**WS-09-02: Capability-Coachability-Fit Assessment**

Think back on a recent hire (or a candidate you've interviewed in the last 6 months). Score them on capability, coachability, and fit using this framework. Then add a fourth column: *What would I have learned if I'd probed their AI habits?*

This isn't about re-evaluating the hire — it's about making the absence of AI screening concrete before Session 5.

---

## Step 3 — One Observation (5 min)

Think about the last person you interviewed or the current open req on your team.

**Ask yourself:** If this candidate told you "I prefer real selling over AI tools," what would your response be? Would you probe further? Move them out? Not know what to do?

You don't need to write anything. Just notice where you have clarity and where you don't. Session 5 gives you the framework for that exact conversation.

---

## What to Bring

- Your WS-09-01 and WS-09-02 completed (even if rough)
- A clear picture of your current 30-60-90 onboarding plan — or an honest acknowledgment that you don't have a formal one
- Any open req or anticipated hire in mind — the workshop works better with a real role to apply it to
- Optional: a candidate you've interviewed recently who gave you a good or bad feeling that you couldn't quite articulate — Session 5 may help you understand why
