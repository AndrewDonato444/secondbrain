## What You're Preparing For

Session 6 is the capstone. We're covering team culture and AI communication, the fairness question, managing up, and the 30/90-day roadmap you're leaving with — the document you're accountable to at the check-in call.

To get the most from tonight, you need to walk in with a clear picture of your team's actual culture — not where you hope it is — and a starting point for your roadmap. The pre-work surfaces both.

This is the last pre-work assignment. Do it honestly.

---

## Step 1 — Read the Foundation (15 min)

Open the Module 10 Foundations Library in the Skool community:  
**Motivation & Culture — Foundations**

Read these two sections:
- **The Five Demotivators** — the framework for what actually drives reps to disengage or leave. You'll use this in the workshop with an addition: AI uncertainty as the sixth demotivator.
- **The Culture Audit** — the gap between stated culture ("we're a high-performance team") and actual culture ("reps don't bring real problems to their manager"). You completed WS-10-01 as part of your pre-work — this gives you the framework behind it.

Also skim: the Inform-Advocate-Execute section in Module 02 (Managing Up). The Week 6 managing-up block builds on this directly.

---

## Step 2 — Complete the Pre-Work Assessments (20 min)

**WS-10-01: What's Your Actual Culture**

Complete this honestly. The question isn't "what do I want the culture to be?" It's "what do reps actually experience when they're on my team?"

Pay specific attention to: what stories do reps tell about the team when you're not there? What do they complain about? What's never raised in 1:1s but shows up in behavior?

**SA-10-02: Demotivator Audit**

Score your team against all five demotivators. Which one is most active right now? Be specific — "lack of clarity" is not an answer. "Lack of clarity about how AI affects their quota targets" is.

**Bring both to Session 6.** The debrief in Block 4 builds directly on your answers.

---

## Step 3 — One Reflection (5–10 min)

Think about the 6 weeks of this program. Before you come to the capstone, answer this:

**What's the one protocol or framework from Weeks 1-5 that you've already used — and what happened?**

Write it down. Even two sentences. You'll use it in the roadmap section of WS-W6-01, and Andrew may ask you to share it in the opening of Session 6.

If you haven't used anything yet: that's also worth noting. What got in the way? What would it take to use it this week, before the session?
