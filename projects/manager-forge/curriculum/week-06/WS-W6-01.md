## What Happens at the 30-Day Check-In Call

Andrew will read your roadmap before the call. The call is structured around three questions:
1. What did you commit to in Week 1? What happened?
2. What's stuck? What do you need to work through?
3. What's your Month 2 focus based on what you've learned?

The call is not a report. It's a coaching conversation — the same kind you've been building with your reps for six weeks.

Come with your roadmap. Come with what's actually happening, not the version you hoped for.
