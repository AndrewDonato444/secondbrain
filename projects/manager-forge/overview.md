## What It Is

A cohort-based program for AI-enabled sales managers. The thesis: sales management is changing and the managers who understand how to lead with AI — not just use it — will pull away from the field.

Uses a **stack model**:
- **AI Spine (core program):** New curriculum built around operating as an AI-enabled sales manager. This is the headline product.
- **Foundations Library (async backstop):** The best of the original 13 modules, available as companion async content. Solid fundamentals, not the pitch.
