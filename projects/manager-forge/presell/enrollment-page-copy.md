## FAQ

**Is this recorded?**
Yes. Sessions are uploaded to the Foundations Library within 24 hours. But the recording isn't the point — the live conversation between managers in the same room is. Recordings are for review, not as a replacement for attending.

**What if I miss a session?**
You get the recording and the takeaway materials. You miss the live conversation. We don't offer makeup sessions. If you know you'll miss more than one session, wait for the next cohort.

**Is this for first-time managers only?**
No. It's most valuable for managers in the first 1-3 years of the role — before the habits are calcified and while the AI transition is still actively underway. If you've been managing for 10 years and haven't updated your approach, you'll probably still get value — but the framing assumes you're navigating a recent shift, not looking back on one.

**I'm already using AI tools. Is this too basic?**
If you're already running an AI-augmented pipeline review, coaching your reps through specific archetypes, and screening candidates for AI fluency with a rubric — yes, probably too basic. If you're doing some of those things informally and want the actual system, no.

**My company doesn't use Gong or Clari. Does this apply?**
Yes. The protocols work with any data source — Salesforce, HubSpot, a spreadsheet. The skill isn't "how to use Gong." It's how to use whatever data you have differently.

**When do sessions run?**
Sundays July 13–August 17, 2026. Time TBD (will be set based on cohort timezone distribution before enrollment closes).

**Is there a refund policy?**
If you enroll and decide it's not right for you before Session 2, full refund. After Session 2, no refund — the cohort experience is built on everyone being present.
