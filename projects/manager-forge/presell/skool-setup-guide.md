## Step 6 — Stripe + Enrollment

**In Skool:**
→ Settings → Payments → Connect Stripe

**Set pricing:**
- Enrollment price: $2,500 (USD, one-time)
- Set community access to begin immediately on payment

**Two-pay option:**
Skool doesn't natively support payment plans. Create a Stripe Payment Link separately:
- Product name: "Manager Forge — Founding Cohort (2-Pay)"
- Price 1: $1,350 on enrollment
- Price 2: $1,350 due 30 days later (use Stripe's subscription with a 1-month interval, set to cancel after 2 payments)
- After payment of first installment, manually add the buyer to Skool (or use Zapier/Make to automate: Stripe payment → Skool invite)

**Enrollment page URL:**
Set a custom Skool URL: skool.com/manager-forge (or closest available)

## Step 8 — Zoom Setup

**If you have Zoom Pro:** Create one recurring Zoom meeting for the 6 sessions.
- Title: "Manager Forge — Founding Cohort"
- Recurring: Weekly, 6 occurrences, starting July 13
- Duration: 90 minutes
- Registration: off (link goes in Skool community, not public)
- Waiting room: on (so you control who enters)
- Recording: set to auto-record to cloud

**If you don't have Zoom Pro:** Add it before July. $15.99/mo. Free tier cuts at 40 minutes.

Post the recurring Zoom link in each Week space the Sunday before that session. Don't post it publicly or it ends up in someone's spam folder with other Zoom links.
